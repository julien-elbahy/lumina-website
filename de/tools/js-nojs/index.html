Why JS vs No-JS matters

Many AI crawlers (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, CCBot) do not execute JavaScript. If your site renders content client-side (React, Vue, Angular), these crawlers may see an empty or incomplete page. This tool shows exactly what's different.
