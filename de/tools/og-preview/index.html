Why Open Graph matters

When someone shares your URL on social media, the platform reads your OG tags to generate a preview card. Without proper tags, platforms show a broken or generic preview.

FAQ

What is the ideal OG image size?+
1200×630 pixels for Facebook and LinkedIn. Twitter uses 1200×628 for summary_large_image cards.
Why doesn't my image show?+
Common causes: relative URL instead of absolute, image blocked by robots.txt, HTTP instead of HTTPS, or the image is too large.
