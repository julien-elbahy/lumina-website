FAQ

Which AI crawlers should I check?+
GPTBot (OpenAI), ClaudeBot (Anthropic), PerplexityBot, Google-Extended (Gemini), Bytespider (TikTok), CCBot, and cohere-ai.
Does blocking remove training data?+
No. robots.txt only prevents future crawling. Contact the AI provider directly to request data removal.
