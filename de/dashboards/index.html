Häufige Fragen

Dashboards Häufige Fragen

Are both dashboards really free?+
Yes. Free, read-only, no Lumina account. The only requirement is an existing Google account with Search Console and/or Analytics 4 access. Your OAuth tokens stay in your browser localStorage.
What's the difference between these and the Lumina Chrome Extension?+
The Chrome Extension embeds both dashboards plus on-page SEO audits, crawler checks, schema validation, and AI tools directly into any browser tab you visit. The web dashboards on this site give you the same data without needing to install anything — perfect when you just want a quick dashboard view on any device.
What permissions do the dashboards need?+
GSC uses the webmasters.readonly scope (read-only Search Console access). GA4 uses the analytics.readonly scope (read-only Analytics 4 access). Both are sensitive scopes — your tokens stay in your browser.
Can I use both dashboards at once?+
Yes. Connect both in Settings, switch between dashboards from the top-level nav, and use Ask AI to cross-reference insights from both data sources at once.
How does the Ask AI integration work?+
Every row in every table has a small Ask AI button. Clicking it opens the Ask AI page with a pre-filled question referencing that specific row. The AI copilot then fetches your full GSC + GA4 context and returns a data-backed answer with concrete next steps.
