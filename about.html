Web Development Lead, SEO & GEO specialist, and the person behind Lumina. Based in Austria.

Why I built Lumina

I work in SEO and web development every day — managing websites, running audits, optimizing for search engines, and increasingly, for AI-powered search. I kept switching between five different browser extensions, browser tabs, and tools just to get a complete picture of a single page.

Lumina started as a personal tool to solve that problem. One extension that gives me everything I need: on-page checks, SERP preview, schema validation, AI crawler analysis, Google Search Console data, GA4 insights, keyword research, and AI-powered content tools.

I made it free because the best SEO tools shouldn't be locked behind expensive subscriptions. Your API keys, your data, your browser — no middleman.
