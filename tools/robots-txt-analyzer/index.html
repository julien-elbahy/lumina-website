FAQ

Which AI crawlers should I check?+
GPTBot (OpenAI), ClaudeBot (Anthropic), PerplexityBot, Google-Extended (Gemini), Bytespider (TikTok), CCBot, and cohere-ai.
Does blocking remove training data?+
No. robots.txt only prevents future crawling. Contact the AI provider directly to request data removal.
What is a robots.txt file?+
robots.txt is a text file at your site's root (example.com/robots.txt) that tells web crawlers which pages they can or cannot access. It uses directives like Allow and Disallow to control crawling behavior.
Can robots.txt block AI crawlers?+
Yes. You can add User-agent rules for specific AI crawlers like GPTBot, ClaudeBot, or Google-Extended and set Disallow: / to block them from crawling your site for training data.
What happens without a robots.txt?+
Without a robots.txt file, all crawlers (including AI bots) assume they have permission to access every page on your site. Adding one gives you explicit control over crawler behavior.
