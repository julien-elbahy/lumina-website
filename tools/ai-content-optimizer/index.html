How it works

Paste your content or fetch from a URL, then choose your target market and keywords. The tool grades your content from A+ to F based on length, readability, keyword usage, and structure. Auto-detect keywords pulls the best terms from your content with real search volumes for your selected market. How does the scoring work?+
The content score combines keyword density (ideal: 1–3%), readability (Flesch-Kincaid), content length (300+ words recommended), and sentence variation. Higher scores indicate better on-page SEO alignment for your target keyword.
Is the AI analysis free?+
Yes — you get 10 free AI queries per day (shared across all AI features). For unlimited use, enter your own OpenAI API key. The client-side analysis (word count, density, readability) always works without limits.
How does the AI optimization work?+
The AI analyzes your content against your target keyword, evaluating keyword placement, semantic relevance, readability, and structure. It then generates specific suggestions to improve on-page SEO without keyword stuffing.
What keywords should I target?+
Choose one primary keyword per page that matches user search intent. Long-tail keywords (3-5 words) typically have less competition. The tool works best when you enter the exact phrase users would type into a search engine.
Does this tool support multiple languages?+
Yes. The readability and keyword density analysis works with any language that uses spaces between words. AI-powered suggestions are available in all languages supported by the underlying model.
