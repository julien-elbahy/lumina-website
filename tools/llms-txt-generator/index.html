FAQ

What's the difference between robots.txt and llms.txt?+
robots.txt controls crawler access. llms.txt provides structured info about your site's purpose and how AI should cite your content.
Do all AI crawlers support llms.txt?+
Adoption is growing. Having one is a forward-looking investment in your GEO strategy.
What is an llms.txt file?+
llms.txt is a structured text file placed at the root of your website that tells AI models about your site's purpose, content structure, and preferred citation format. It helps AI systems represent your brand accurately.
Which AI crawlers use llms.txt?+
Major AI systems including ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity are increasingly supporting llms.txt. The specification is being adopted as a standard way for websites to communicate with AI models.
How is llms.txt different from robots.txt?+
robots.txt controls whether crawlers can access your pages. llms.txt provides context about your site so AI models can better understand and cite your content. They serve complementary purposes.
