How to use

Enter your URL, title, and description. The preview updates as you type. The pixel counter shows if Google will truncate your snippet. Use Fetch from URL to pull current values from any live page.

Optimal lengths

Google truncates by pixel width. Keep titles under ~60 chars (~580px) and descriptions under ~155 chars (~920px). Wide characters (W, M) take more space than narrow ones (i, l).

FAQ

What is a SERP preview tool?+
It simulates how your page appears in Google search results — title, URL, description — helping you optimize for click-through rate.
Why does pixel width matter?+
Google truncates based on pixel width, not character count. Wide characters get cut sooner. This tool measures actual pixels.
Does Google always use my title?+
Not always. Google may rewrite if it thinks its version better matches the query. Well-optimized meta tags are more likely used as-is.
