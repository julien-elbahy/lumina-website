Why Open Graph matters

When someone shares your URL on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, or Slack, the platform reads your Open Graph (and Twitter Card) meta tags to generate a preview card. Without proper OG tags, platforms may show a broken or generic preview — leading to lower engagement.

Key tags: og:title, og:description, og:image (1200×630px recommended), og:url, twitter:card.

FAQ

What is the ideal OG image size?+
1200×630 pixels for Facebook and LinkedIn. Twitter uses 1200×628 for summary_large_image cards. Keep file size under 5MB.
Why doesn't my image show?+
Common causes: relative URL instead of absolute, image blocked by robots.txt, HTTP instead of HTTPS, incorrect MIME type, or the image is too large.
