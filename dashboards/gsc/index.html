Why a second GSC UI

Everything Google's GSC could be — in one screen

Native Search Console is powerful but slow, hides context behind clicks, and has no AI follow-up. Lumina loads clicks, impressions, CTR, position, winners, losers, cannibalization, and low-hanging fruit in a single scroll — and every row launches a data-backed AI analysis.

🏆 Winners & losers
  • Queries whose average position improved vs previous period
  • Queries that dropped (sorted by biggest loss)
  • Click deltas next to each row
  • Ask AI about any movement
🍎 Low-hanging fruit
  • Queries in positions 5–25 with high impressions
  • Sorted by impressions/position ratio
  • Instant CTR gain potential
  • Perfect backlog for content updates
⚔️ Cannibalization

FAQ

Google Search Console dashboard FAQ

How is this different from native Search Console?+
Native GSC is slow, requires many clicks to compare periods, and has no winners/losers, no low-hanging fruit, no cannibalization detection, and no AI follow-up. Lumina loads all of that in one scroll.
Is the dashboard really free?+
Yes. Free and read-only. The only requirement is an existing Google account with Search Console access. No Lumina account, no subscription, no limits.
What data does Lumina access?+
Only the webmasters.readonly OAuth scope — enough to read your GSC Search Analytics data. Lumina cannot modify anything, cannot access other Google services, and your OAuth tokens stay in your browser localStorage on your device.
How does the Ask AI button work?+
Every row in every table has a small button. Clicking it opens the Lumina Ask AI page with a pre-filled question that references that specific row. The AI copilot then fetches your full GSC context + live SERP data and returns a data-backed answer.
Which date ranges work?+
7 days, 28 days, 3 months, 6 months, 12 months, and a custom date range picker. Google's Search Console API returns a maximum of 16 months of historical data.
Can I switch between GSC properties?+
Yes. The property dropdown at the top of the dashboard lists all properties from your connected Google account. Switch any time — the dashboard reloads with fresh data.
